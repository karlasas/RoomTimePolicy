## **RoomTimer Privacy Policy** **Last Updated: [09/28/2025]** ### **Introduction** RoomTimer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Apple Watch application. ### **Information We Collect** **We do not collect any personal information.** RoomTimer is designed with privacy in mind. The app operates entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. **Local Data Only:** - Room configurations and settings - Timer durations and session data - Notification preferences - App usage data (stored locally on your device) ### **How We Use Information** All data collected by RoomTimer is used solely for the app's core functionality: - Managing room timers and sessions - Providing notifications and alerts - Storing your custom settings and preferences - Maintaining app functionality between sessions ### **Data Storage and Security** **Local Storage Only:** - All data is stored locally on your Apple Watch and iPhone - No data is transmitted to external servers - No cloud storage or backup services are used - Data remains under your complete control **Security Measures:** - Data is protected by Apple's built-in device security - No third-party access to your information - No data sharing with external parties ### **Third-Party Services** RoomTimer does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app operates independently without external data collection. ### **Children's Privacy** RoomTimer is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. ### **Data Retention** Your data is retained only as long as you use the app. When you delete the app, all associated data is removed from your device. ### **Your Rights** Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information: - Access your data through the app interface - Modify or delete settings at any time - Remove all data by deleting the app ### **Changes to This Policy** We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on our website. Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes. ### **Compliance** This Privacy Policy complies with: - Apple App Store Review Guidelines - General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) ### **Summary** RoomTimer is a privacy-first application that: - ✅ Collects no personal information - ✅ Stores all data locally on your device - ✅ Shares no data with third parties - ✅ Requires no internet connection - ✅ Provides complete user control over data Your privacy is our priority. RoomTimer is designed to help you manage your time without compromising your personal information.
